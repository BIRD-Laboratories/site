About Canary ARC

Canary ARC is the AI research division of BirdL, operating as a compact development lab based in Palm Desert, California.

We focus on experimental approaches to:

  • Human-AI communication systems
  • Open-source AI tooling
  • Accessible machine learning applications

Our work is documented transparently on GitHub, with an emphasis on creating reusable components and clear documentation.
